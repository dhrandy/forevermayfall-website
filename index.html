ABOUT THE BAND

Founded in September 2017 by Jason (Guitar) and Jillyan (Singer), Forever May Fall breaks through the genre barriers by combining alternative rock, metal, and numerous other styles to create a unique sound. With the introduction of Pat on bass, Justin on guitar, and Adam on drums, Forever May Fall has become a force to be reckoned with. Deep lyrics, cutting vocals, catchy guitars, melodic bass lines and powerful drums bring stories and moods to life.
